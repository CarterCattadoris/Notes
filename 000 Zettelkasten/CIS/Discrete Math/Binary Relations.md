A **binary relation** from set A to set B is a subset of the [[Cartesian Product]] $A \times B$. 

## Example:
- $W=\{ 1,3,5 \}$  and $Y=\{ a,b \}$
	- $\{ (1,a),(1,b)(3,a),(5,b) \}$
	- $\{ (3,a) \}$
	- $\emptyset$ 
	- $W\times Y$ 
	- There are $|2^{W\times Y}|=2^{|W\times Y|}=2^6$ relations
