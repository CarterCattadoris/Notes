R is symmetric provided for all $x,y \in A$, if $(x,y) \in R$, then $(y,x) \in R$

$R_{1} = \{(1, 1), (1, 3), (3, 5), (3, 1), (5, 3)\}$
$R_{2} = \{(3, 1), (5, 7), (7, 5), (5, 5), (7, 7)\}$
$R_{3} = \{(1, 1), (1, 3), (3, 1), (3, 3), (5, 5), (7, 7)\}$
$R_{4} = \{(1, 3), (3, 5), (1, 5)\}$
$R_{5} = \{ \}$
$R_{6} = A × A$

Symmetric:
- $R_1$
- $R_3$  
- $R_{5}$
- $R_{6}$

Others: 
- $(3, 1) ∈ R2$ but $(1, 3) \not\in R2$ 
- $(3, 5) ∈ R4$ but ($5, 3) \not\in R4$
