Let A be a set. The power set of A(written $2^A$ in our text, often $\rho(A)$ elsewhere) is the set whose elements are the subset of A
$$2^A = \{ S : S \subseteq A \}$$

## Examples:
- $2^{\{ 3,5,8 \}}$ = {$\emptyset$, {3}, {5}, {8}, {3, 5}, {5, 8}, {3, 8}, {3, 5, 8}}
