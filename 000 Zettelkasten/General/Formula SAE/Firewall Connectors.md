### Power Plug
#### inner 
h - sensor 0v B (coolant temperature sensor)
z - sensor 5v B (gear position pullup)
b - sensor 0v B (gear position)
d - PDM output 2 (injector 1)
f - PDM output 2 (injector 2)

#### outer
A - PDM A_3 (injector 3)
B - PDM A_3 (injector 4)
C - sensor 5v B (intake pressure sensor)
D - sensor 0v B (intake pressure sensor)
E - sensor 0v B (intake temp sensor)
F - sensor 5v B (TPS)
G - sensor 0v B (TPS)
H - sensor 5v B (oil pressure sensor)
J - sensor 0v B (oil pressure sensor)
K - sensor 0v B (oil temp sensor)
L - PDM output 2 (coil 1)
M - PDM output 2 (coil 2)
N - PDM output 2 (coil 3)
P - PDM output 2 (coil 4)

#### inner
##### 20 gauge
h - sensor 0v B (crank position sensor)
z - crank position sensor
b - sensor 0v B (cam position sensor)
d - cam position sensor
f - sensor 5v B (cam position sensor)

i - PDM output 9 (LTC Module)

g - battery - (fuel pump)

E - injector 2

G - intake temp sensor
H - injector 3
J - injector 4
K - TPS
L - oil pressure sensor
M - oil temp sensor
N - can lo (LTC Module)
